LionsLink – Connecting Emerson College’s Graduate Student Community
LionsLink is an app concept developed to bridge the gap between Emerson College graduate students and campus life. Recognizing the challenges graduate students face, like off-campus living and irregular schedules, this app is designed to make event discovery, networking, and community-building more accessible. Beyond just event listings, LionsLink provides a platform for students to connect, share resources, and stay informed about academic, networking, and recreational opportunities.
Project Details / Background
The primary goal of LionsLink is to foster a tighter, more engaged community among graduate students. Managed by the Graduate Activities and Networking Organization (GANO), LionsLink serves as a hub for event notifications, networking tools, and a community forum, aiming to keep students connected and involved despite their busy lives.
Key Features:
  • Events Interface: Organized modules like "Hot Events," "You Might Like," "What's New," and "Your Schedule" make event discovery personalized and user-friendly.
  • Networking Profiles: Graduate students can build profiles, connect with peers, and exchange contact information, making networking seamless.
  • Community Forum: A discussion space where students can share employment opportunities, discuss courses, and recommend resources.
